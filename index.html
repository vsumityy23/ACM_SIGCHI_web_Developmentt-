ACM SIGCHI IIT Kanpur Student Chapter

Join us to explore Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience

Welcome to the ACM SIGCHI IIT Kanpur chapter's website! We are a student-led initiative at IIT Kanpur dedicated to making Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) more accessible and intuitive. Our interdisciplinary community organizes conferences, seminars, workshops, and networking events, bringing together students and faculty from diverse fields such as AI/ML, AR/VR, design, computer science, and robotics. We invite anyone interested in HCI to collaborate with us and develop real-world solutions using computing technologies..

Featuring

Saathi

The Saathi app, developed by Shashwat Gupta under the guidance of Prof. Anveshna Srivastava at IIT Kanpur, engages young learners with reading, writing, and arithmetic through gamified learning. Created for local village students, it ensures accessible education via engaging educational games.
